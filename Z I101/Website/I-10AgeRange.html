Description of the data

This bar chart illustrates the percentage of students employed across different age ranges. The data shows that 39.9% of students in the 16-24 age group are employed, the lowest of the four groups. In the 30-39 age group, 45.4% of students are employed, which is slightly lower than the 25-29 group but still a significant proportion. The 40-49 age group has an employment rate of 43.6%.
