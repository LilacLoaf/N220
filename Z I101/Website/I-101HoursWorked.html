Description of the data

For the less than 10 hours category, 3.1% of male students and 4.3% of female students report working in this range. In the 10-19 hours range, 9.1% of males and 10.6% of females work, indicating a slightly higher proportion of females in this category. When it comes to the 20-34 hours range, both genders show similar figures, with 16.6% of male students and 16.3% of female students working these hours. .

This data is important because it gives students a visual idea of how many hours other students are working realistically while still attending school. While looking at this data they can start planning their own work/school schedule hours based on others experience.
