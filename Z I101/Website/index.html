Description of Data set

Our dataset covers College Student Employment from the perspective of Race, Age, and the amount of time per week that students work. We want to cover this dataset because our school of IUI is known as a place for working students, which is reflected with many of our group members. We hope that this website reassures people who are on the fence about returning to their higher education by showing them that there are many who are going to college across the nation who are in the same boat as them.

Data Source

Our dataset was sourced from the National Center for Educational Statistics (NCES). The NCES creates annual reports to gauge the Condition of Education. This is done to provide data on a national level, as well as by researchers, the media, and the general public.

We used a library called Chart.js to help us create our charts, and present our data in a more visually pleasing way.
