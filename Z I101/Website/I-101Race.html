Description of the data

This bar chart displays the population distribution of students by race, illustrating the percentage of students in four racial categories: White, Black, Hispanic, and Asian. White students make up the largest proportion of the student population, at 44.1%, followed by Hispanic students, who account for 39.0%. The percentage of Black students is 36.6%, while Asian students represent 31.1% of the population.

This is important for future students who are curious about the race population as well as representation in their respected population. Hopefully this shows that no matter the race, anyone can study and work. Hopefully, this encourages them to register for that class or apply for a job if unsure.
